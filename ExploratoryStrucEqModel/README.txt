Scripts to conduct Exploratory Structural EQ modelling.

Method isnt very popular (yet) but basically it has some benefits from EFA as it allows modelling of item cross loadings on other components than solely the item where the item mainly / mostly contributes to.
Good intro paper written is Asparouhov, Muthén & Muthén; https://www.statmodel.com/download/EFACFA810.pdf
See statmodel.com for more information.
