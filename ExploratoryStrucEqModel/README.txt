Scripts to conduct Exploratory Structural EQ modelling.

Method isnt very popular (yet) but basically it has some benefits from Confirmatory Factor Analyses as it allows item loadings on other components than only the component (factor) where the item mainly / mostly contributes to in terms of explained variation (squared item loading).
Good intro paper written is Asparouhov, Muthén & Muthén; https://www.statmodel.com/download/EFACFA810.pdf
See statmodel.com for more information.
