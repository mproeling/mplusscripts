Scripts for Factor Mixture Modeling (F2C2-5) and Latent Class Analyses.

F2C2-5 for fitting a model with 2 specified factors (from initial factor analyses) and 2-5 latent classes.
Short report / abstract of the outcomes published by Roeling et al. in Tijdschrift Kindergeneeskunde, in 2012.
One of the best overviews on this stuff is written by drs Lubke and Muthén in SEM: Investigating population heterogeneity with factor 
mixture models in 2005.
